This is a flight sim setup for geofs that you can plug into your device and then it should automatically set everything up.
The bottom joystick is the control (roll and elevation) and the other joystick is your rudder and throttle.
The buttons top to bottom landing gear, flaps, airbrake, brake and trim, when you press the trim button it will (you'le never guess) trim your throttle permanently until you pres it again.
The joysticks are just general joysticks you'le find in drone controllers and playstation controller (you can hall effect you're controllers and use the old joysticks for this).
